Activities

It would be a sin to spend a day staying within four walls when the weather is nice - surrounding nature offers shorter or longer trails, cycle routes (if you are out of training, you can hire an electrobike). You can find tennis court, basketball hoop and foot tennis court on the premises. When your surroundings are this beautiful, nothing keeps you from going back in time - to enjoy horse riding at nearby farm.

Nearby forests amaze you by the amount of wildlife - and if you are an owner of hunting licence, nothing keeps you from hunting depending on the season. Season of pig-slaughter does not avoid our pension and you can enjoy the right atmosphere with master slaughterer and become his helper.

During the time when you relax, your children can enjoy themselves at certified children's corner, trampoline, by watching fairy tales or playing various games.

For others we have an andrenaline choice, to try shooting a stationary target or clay pigeon shooting at our shooting range.

  • E-Bike
  • Horse riding
  • Tennis
  • Football tennis
  • Hunting
  • Shooting range (targets, clay pigeons)
  • Pig slaughtering
